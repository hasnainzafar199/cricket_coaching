About Us

Brooklyn Cricket Training Club in New York, USA has been established to teach youngsters the skills of the game and to inspire them with its foundations - team work, honesty, integrity and fair play. Our vision is to create a center of excellence for young talented cricketers who want to take their game to the highest level. We believe the best way to achieve that is to show the young players how we prepare for games, both on and off the field. Who is BCTC for

Brooklyn Cricket Training Club is for boys and girls aged between 5-19 years old. Whether it is your first experience of cricket or you are a budding player, you will have fun, improve and be challenged at the Academy. Players are grouped based on age, ability and experience and are assessed on the first day of their coaching to make sure that they are challenged to the appropriate level. The BCTC coaching program is designed to build on natural talent and unearth future stars. All our staff are certified coaches and go through our in-house training.



Our Facilities

As part of its commitment to our vision, the Queens United Cricket Academy boasts facilities that are among the best across the USA.Our home ground, Marine Cricket Ground is located close to Kings Plaza & about 20 miles from Manhattan. The Academy is accessible by car or bus service.The unrivaled facilities include:

  • Full size, well maintained ground with two pitches
  • One all-weather cement pitch practice cage
  • One matting wicket practice cage
  • Indoor winter training sessions.
  • Sight-screens, storage container and bleachers
